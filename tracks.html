F1

Tracks

There are three types of tracks, street, road, and race circuits. Street circuits are held on closed city streets. Road circuits are held on a combination of closed city streets and permanent tracks. Race circuits are held on permanent tracks. There are currently no road circuits in F1 and have not been used since 1972.
